About Me

Yikes! Talking about myself like this in a serious way makes me uncomfotable, so I make jokes. I will attempt to keep it to a minimum. My name is Anne Palangi (I probably should have led with that). I am a former English teacher who worked primarily in Richmond Public Schools. I have a deep reserve of patience and understanding, and the ability to communicate with just about anyone. As far as hobbies go, I enjoy a lot of different activities: baking, cross stitching (both subversive and the kind you can show grandma), making jewelry (I'm working a lot in silver currently), and playing guitar very, very poorly. I'm also a Hufflepuff and an Aquarius.
